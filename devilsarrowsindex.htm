Devils Arrows

Map Ref:

Despite being right by the side of the A1(M) Motorway these three enormous standing stones still manage to evoke a sense of wonder and otherworldliness. It is an odd feeling to be confronted by two completely different testaments to mankinds control of the landscape- the standing stones and the motorway, and I was surprised by the way that one appeared completely undisturbed by the other.

The calm by the side of the A1 storm.
